[Box display](https://discord.com/channels/1375556143186837695/1454753497638506689/1454753498494144522) that places the 1st [box](https://discord.com/channels/1375556143186837695/1454806018348290109/1454806020177133708) when you click the noteblock, reducing size and manual effort.
## Features
- Fully hopper locked, minimum hoppers
- Fast replacement with clean animation
- Easy and compact 1st box placement by clicking the noteblock
## Considerations
- Honey and slime
- Noteblock may need 2 clicks to place the 1st box
- Boxes are buffered in the accessible dropper/hopper
## Notes
[Box collection](https://discord.com/channels/1375556143186837695/1454805930670559326/1454805932532830268) isn'[t](https://discord.com/channels/1375556143186837695/1454753634435858666/1454753635690090539) 100% reliable in the [schematic](https://discord.com/channels/1375556143186837695/1454811558009110704/1454811560626356487) but can be made reliable with this layout without hopperlocking the collection hoppers.
