A device that moves items at a rate of 10 blocks per second, allows for timing redstone signals with item movement, and uses a slime/piston pusher to shoot items into the stream at 10 BPS starting at the first water block.
## Features
- Moves items at 10 blocks per second
- Allows for timing redstone signals with item movement
- Uses slime/piston pusher to shoot items at 10BPS starting at the first water block
- Average motion is 0.499750482
- Tested for 1600 blocks
## Notes
Item should be aligned against the slime block.
