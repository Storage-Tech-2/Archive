Based on Yams v2.3 Item Layout.
A reworked layout that aims to add missing things, but tries to keep the original in mind
## Features
- 1 bulk hall
- 2 chest halls
- 16 MIS Categories
## Exceptions
- Bee Nests
- Player and mob heads
- Dragon eggs
- Suspicious gravel/sand
- Ominous trial keys
- Heavy cores
## Additions compared to original
- Bamboo and its variants
- Lightning rods and their variants
- Copper golems and their variants
- Pressure plates and buttons
- Paintings
- Rotten Flesh
- Tipped arrows
## Notes
The schematic contains both a plane and a spinal version. The skeletal spine version represents Yams v2 filters.
## Compatibility
1.21.11+
